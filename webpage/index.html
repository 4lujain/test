To Leading Universities and Acadimic Institutions.

We are excited to invite you to the "CCH-2025" Technology Innovation Hackathon, the largest event designed to empower university students in showcasing their creativity and applying their technical skills through AI-based projects.

At "CCH-2025," we recognize the importance of fostering innovation. The winning teams will receive the following cash prizes:

First Prize

20,000 Jordanian Dinars.

Second Prize

15,000 Jordanian Dinars.

Third Prize

10,000 Jordanian Dinars.

Preparatory Month

The preparatory month for the hackathon lasts 30 days and serves as a crucial stage focused on

Virtual Workshops

A series of online workshops, led by experts in AI, modern technologies, and project management, will be held to help participants learn how to effectively apply AI in their projects, focusing on key sectors highlighted in the competition

Expert Support and Guidance

Teams will have the chance to interact directly with specialized consultants and experts, receiving personalized guidance to help design and develop their projects based on real-world needs.

Idea Development and Evaluation

Each team will present their project idea at the start of the preparatory month. A specialized panel will assess the initial ideas and offer feedback for improvement, allowing teams to refine and enhance their concepts for the final competition.

Brainstorming Sessions

Regular brainstorming sessions will be conducted to encourage teams to think creatively and explore innovative technical solutions to real-world challenges within the relevant sectors.
